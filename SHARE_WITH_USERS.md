## 🔧 Step 2: Install (2 minutes)

### Chrome / Edge / Brave:

1. **Extract the ZIP file** you downloaded
2. **Open your browser** and go to:
   - Chrome: `chrome://extensions/`
   - Edge: `edge://extensions/`
   - Brave: `brave://extensions/`
3. **Turn ON "Developer mode"** (toggle in top-right)
4. **Click "Load unpacked"**
5. **Select the `browser-extension` folder** from the extracted files
6. **Pin the extension** (click puzzle icon 🧩, then pin)

✅ Done! You should see the extension icon in your toolbar.

---

## 🚀 Step 3: Connect (2 minutes)

1. **Click the extension icon** in your toolbar
2. **Click "Open Dashboard →"**
3. **Sign up** with your email (free!)
4. **Return to the extension** - it's now connected!

✅ You're all set!

## 💼 Step 4: Start Tracking!

### It's Automatic!

1. **Browse job sites** like normal:
   - LinkedIn Jobs

2. **See the magic happen**:
